How it works

Three steps. No storage-unit weekends.

We handle the bins, the pickup, and the truck. You decide what to store and when to have it back.

1

We drop off bins

Order as many as you need through the app. The first delivery is free. The bins are ours — sturdy, sealable, and stackable — you just fill them at your own pace.

2

You pack, we pick up

When you're ready, schedule a pickup window. A driver comes to your door and loads everything. No lugging to a storage unit across town.

3

Call things back anytime

Need your holiday decor? Your kid's winter coat? Camping gear for the weekend? Schedule a return in the app and we bring the bin back to you, usually next day.

Common questions

Things people ask first.

What if I need something immediately?

Same- or next-day delivery is included — schedule a return in the app and we'll bring your bin back on the next available window. No rush fees.

How do you keep my things safe?

Every bin is tagged with a QR code and scanned at every handoff — pickup, arrival at the facility, shelf placement, and return — so there's a full custody trail. Storage is climate-controlled. Contents are insured up to $2,000 per bin.

Can I send bins back if I don't need them anymore?

Yes, any time. If a bin is empty at your home, tap "Send it back" in the app and we'll retrieve it at no cost. Your monthly fee for that bin stops at the next billing cycle.

What counts as a "bin"?

A standard 27-gallon heavy-duty tote, about the size of a large moving box. We provide them — you don't need to supply your own containers.
